UOL GPA & CGPA Calculator

This UOL GPA & CGPA Calculator is designed for students of the University of Lahore. It follows the official UOL grading system and allows accurate calculation of semester GPA and cumulative CGPA using credit-weighted formulas.

How GPA Is Calculated at UOL

GPA is calculated by dividing total quality points by total credit hours in a semester. Each grade carries a specific grade point value as defined by UOL.

Difference Between GPA and CGPA

GPA represents academic performance in a single semester, while CGPA reflects overall performance across all semesters at the University of Lahore.



Frequently Asked Questions about GPA & CGPA at UOL

To get a degree from The University of Lahore (UOL), you generally need a minimum CGPA of 2.00 for undergraduate (BS) programs and 2.50 for postgraduate (MS/MPhil) programs, though specific programs might have higher requirements, often around 2.50 CGPA for Master's/Ph.D. admissions; check your specific program's admission guide as requirements can vary. For Undergraduate (BS) Degrees: Minimum CGPA: 2.00 out of 4.00. For Postgraduate (MS/MPhil/Master's) Degrees: Standard Requirement: 2.50 out of 4.00 CGPA or 60% marks in your previous degree. Program Variations: Some programs might have stricter entry criteria, like 2.50 CGPA for Computer Science/IT. Key Points to Remember: Program-Specific: Always check the official Admission Guide for your specific program (e.g., CS, Law, Business) for precise CGPA requirements, as they can differ slightly. HEC Guidelines: UOL follows HEC guidelines for grading and conversion. Admission vs. Graduation: Note that admission requirements (e.g., 2.50 for Master's) can be higher than the minimum to complete the degree (e.g., 2.00 for BS).
